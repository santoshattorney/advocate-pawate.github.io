About

Experienced Legal Counsel in Bengaluru

With over two decades of legal practice in Bengaluru, Attorney Santosh Pawate has built a reputation for diligent representation, clear communication, and client-first advocacy. Enrolled with the Bar Council of Karnataka, he has appeared before district courts, high courts, and tribunals across the state.

His practice spans civil litigation, criminal defence, and property law — giving clients comprehensive legal support under one roof. He is known for thorough case preparation, honest counsel, and accessible guidance throughout every stage of proceedings.
